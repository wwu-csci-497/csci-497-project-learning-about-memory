To recap

We learned about the capacity of working memory. Then we learned about chunking.


Finally, we took a quiz. It wasn't very hard, but that wasn't the point. The point is that recalling information is incredibly important to learning.


Instead of cramming and using repetition, it's much better to think deeply and make meaningful connections, studying in short sessions, and then recall through a self administered quiz. As long as the questions aren't too easy and you aren't testing what you already know easily, you'll be able to remember things much better.
