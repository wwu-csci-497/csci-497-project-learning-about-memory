This is Chunking

Whereas in the first experiment you had to remember each digit by itself, for this one you could "group" them together. Chunk them, so that you don't have to remember any individual letter, but only the single grouping.

This was made easier for you too, with the spaces between each word. If you're not a citizen of the United States or aren't fluent in English you may still have trouble.

If you are, you've probably noticed that the letters given to you all spelled three letter words or acronyms.

This was done to help you chunk the letters together, into groups of meaning.

Chunking information into similar groups or categories allows you to remember more. In short-term memory, and long-term.

It'll be incredibly helpful to you when learning and retaining new information.
