Here's how this works

On your screen will slowly appear a bunch of numbers. Your goal is to recite the numbers after they disappear.

The only tool that you have is your own memory, so no writing down the numbers as you see it! Don't worry though, you'll get one second for each digit. Surely that'll be enough time, right? ;)

Initially, the list will be short, but it'll get longer as you progress. How many digits can you remember?
